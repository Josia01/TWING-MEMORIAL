Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 1985 in memory of Dr. Jonathan Twing, our institution is dedicated to providing accessible, quality education that nurtures both academic excellence and strong moral character.

We believe every child deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and reach their full potential. Our holistic approach combines rigorous academics with character development, sports, arts, and community service.

Our graduates have gone on to excel in universities worldwide and make meaningful contributions to society across various fields.
