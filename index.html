About Our School

Welcome to Sunrise Academy

Founded in 1995, Sunrise Academy has been committed to providing a comprehensive and challenging educational experience that prepares students for success in college and beyond.

Our dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and diverse curriculum create an environment where students can explore their interests, develop critical thinking skills, and grow as individuals.

We believe in educating the whole child - academically, socially, emotionally, and physically - to become responsible global citizens.
