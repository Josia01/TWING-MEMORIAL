About Our Institution

With over 35 years of educational excellence, Twing Memorial Schools has been a pillar of quality education in our community.

Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 1985 in memory of Dr. Jonathan Twing, our school is dedicated to providing accessible, quality education that nurtures both academic excellence and strong moral character.

We believe every child deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and reach their full potential regardless of their family's financial situation.

Our holistic approach to education combines rigorous academics with character development, sports, arts, and community service.
