About Twing Memorial

Founded in memory of Dr. Jonathan Twing, our institution has been a cornerstone of educational excellence for over three decades.

Where Education Meets Inspiration

Twing Memorial Schools is a premier educational institution offering comprehensive programs from elementary through high school. Our holistic approach balances academic rigor with character development.

With a student-teacher ratio of 15:1, we ensure personalized attention and a supportive learning environment for every child.

Our Academic Programs

Comprehensive educational pathways designed to meet the needs of every student from early childhood through adolescence.

Elementary School

Our elementary program (Grades K-5) focuses on foundational skills, curiosity, and social development in a nurturing environment.

  • Interactive learning centers
  • Literacy and numeracy foundations
  • Arts and music integration
  • Physical education and health
  • Social-emotional learning
  • Technology integration
Elementary Details

High School

Our high school program (Grades 9-12) offers college preparatory curriculum with diverse electives and extracurricular opportunities.

  • Advanced Placement (AP) courses
  • STEM specialization tracks
  • College counseling services
  • Career and technical education
  • Arts and humanities programs
  • Athletics and clubs

Our Campus Facilities

Modern learning environments designed to inspire creativity, collaboration, and academic excellence.

Modern Library & Media Center

Our library features over 20,000 volumes, digital resources, and collaborative study spaces for students of all ages.

Advanced Science Laboratories

Fully-equipped labs for biology, chemistry, and physics with modern equipment for hands-on scientific exploration.

Athletic Complex

Comprehensive sports facilities including gymnasium, swimming pool, soccer field, and tennis courts.
