Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our sponsorship program

How long does a sponsorship last?

Sponsorships typically last for one academic year but can be renewed annually. Many sponsors continue their support until their sponsored child graduates. You can choose to sponsor for one year, multiple years, or until graduation.

Can I communicate with my sponsored child?

Yes! Sponsors receive updates, photos, and letters from their sponsored child twice a year. You can also send letters and cards to your sponsored child through our secure portal. We encourage this meaningful connection.

What does my sponsorship payment cover?

Your sponsorship covers the full cost of the child's education including school fees, uniforms, textbooks, supplies, daily nutritious lunch, basic medical checkups, and extracurricular activity fees. 100% of sponsorship funds go directly to the child's education.

Can I cancel my sponsorship?

Yes, you can cancel your sponsorship at any time by contacting our sponsorship team. We ask for 30 days notice so we can find another sponsor for the child. Your sponsorship will not auto-renew if you cancel before the renewal date.

Is my sponsorship tax-deductible?

Yes, Twing Memorial Schools is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. All sponsorship payments are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law. You will receive a tax receipt via email after each payment.
